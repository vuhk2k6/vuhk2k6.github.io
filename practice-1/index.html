Impressive graphics

The scene system in the game is diverse according to each type of battle, with separate graphics. Although the scenery in the game has quite a lot of details such as rivers, mountains, water, houses, ... but if you look closely, they still lack realism, a bit blurry.

General comments

AOV is an impressive product on the mobile platform, with a unique MOBA style. The game owns a diverse general system with many different playstyles, creating a product "miniature League of Legends". In particular, the champion in the game is a collection of many heroes, from Greek mythology, the story of the Three Kingdoms, to fairy tales, ... creating a variety and interesting of the product.
